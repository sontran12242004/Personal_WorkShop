**On this page**, you will need to introduce your worklog. **How** did you complete it? How many weeks did you take to complete the program? **What** did you do in those weeks?

Typically, and as a standard, a worklog is carried out over about 3 months (throughout the internship period) with weekly contents as follows:

**Week 1:** [Getting familiar with AWS and basic AWS services](1.1-week1/)

**Week 2:** [Understanding Amazon VPC and network components](1.2-week2/)

**Week 3:** [Deep dive into Amazon EC2 and storage services](1.3-week3/)

**Week 4:** [Learning Amazon S3 and storage services](1.4-week4/)

**Week 5:** [Understanding AWS Security and IAM](1.5-week5/)

**Week 6:** [Learning AWS Database Services](1.6-week6/)

**Week 7:** [AWS Console and CLI Practice](1.7-week7/)

**Week 8:** [AWS VPC and Network Security](1.8-week8/)

**Week 9:** [Infrastructure Design for Backend Project](1.9-week9/)

**Week 10:** [AWS Services Integration (IAM, S3, SES, Secrets Manager)](1.10-week10/)

**Week 11:** [Backend Optimization and Integration Testing](1.11-week11/)

**Week 12:** [Project Completion and Documentation](1.12-week12/)
